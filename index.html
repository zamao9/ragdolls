about ragdolls pride cattery

Breeders of Ragdolls since 2020, we excel at producing not only beautiful, but healthy and well socialized Ragdoll kittens. We offer the opportunity to adopt beautiful Ragdolls with a variety of colors and patterns. We strive to produce Ragdolls with the deepest ocean blue eyes, bunny soft coats and most loving dispositions. All kittens are screened and tested negative for HCM and health guaranteed. All of our kittens are raised in our home and treated as cherished members of our families until they are ready to find their owners. We strongly believe that this method of raising our Ragdolls is shown in their personality. Our kittens and adults cats are well socialized due to their constant contact with our family including dogs and children.

Every member of our Ragdoll breeding family is picked carefully for health, quality and beauty. All new Ragdolls that are added to our family are quarantined before being introduced to their other family members. Our male cats remain separate from the females where they each enjoy their own room and regular visits. The girls remain together until they are in heat, at which point they are paired with a male and placed together for a few days. Once pregnant, the female is brought into the nursery where she will have their kittens. Kittens are raised in this quiet and peaceful environment. At eight weeks old they are mature enough to have free run about the house and socialize with our family. They remain with us until approximately 14 weeks of age, at which time they are ready to be adopted and taken to their new homes.
